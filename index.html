The Chain

Release: 15 Jul 2015
Made using Construct Classic

Did you ever imagined yourself as a tiger? It's always search for a prey, always running to get food, to leave posterity.
Did you ever imagined yourself as a deer? I'd tell you it's even crappier...
The Chain is a life simulator centered around a small food chain. There are only few animals yet: only mouse, deer and tiger. And two kinds of plants: bushes and wheat. Yet.
You can create/destroy all creatures and plants on small area, watch them grow, feast, kill (especially wheat, WHEAT KILLS....your fps...it'll eat your fps like tiger eats mice MWAHAHAHA)
Watching how food chain works is always...relaxing. So take a mug with fresh coffee, run the game, lean back and watch dem deer suffer from cat killing sprees all the living creatures.

Controls:
Arrows to move around
Wheel to zoom
LMB to drag and drop animals around
LMB/RMB on stats on left upper corner to decrease/increase nimber of existing objects

GTA60

Release date: 2 Jun 2015 (Unfinished)
Made using Construct Classic

GTA60 is a TDS arcade, you play as Cidoji (CJ) who's driving purple car (o_o)
I tried to make graphic more next-gen, but it turned out I can draw only pixel-art, so...
Game is a bit with Russian language, but the main things you need to know:

R - restart level
Arrows - control the vehicle
Green shit (Mary calls it swamp) - throws you into previous level
Spikes (mills) - just to make it more difficult, you know...
Flying faces (Jakubovitch) - (hey-hey Russian players^^)
Turrets - make you regret you didn't know those blue blocks can be pushed around with mouse

Also there are invisible blocks, areas that make you smaller, shotcuts and cheats....fun...no, I promise!
