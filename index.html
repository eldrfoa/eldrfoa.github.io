GTA60

Release date: 2 Jun 2015 (Unfinished)

GTA60 is a TDS arcade, you play as CJ who's driving a purple car (o_o)
I tried to make the graphics more next-gen, but it turned out I can only do pixel-art, so...
Game has a bit of Russian language, but don't worry, all you have to know:

R - restart level
Arrows - control the vehicle
Green shit - throws you into the previous level
Spikes (mills) - just to make it more difficult, you know...
Flying faces (Jakubovitch) - annoying...(hey-hey Russian players^^)
Turrets - make you regret you didn't know those blue blocks can be pushed around with the cursor

Also there are invisible blocks, areas that make you smaller, shotcuts and cheats....fun...no, I promise!
